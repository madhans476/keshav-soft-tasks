About Orbiq

Built by people who were done copy-pasting between apps

Orbiq started as an internal tool at a 12-person support team. Three years later, it's the layer that keeps 1,200+ teams' tools talking to each other.

Our story

Automation shouldn't require an engineering team

We watched operations teams build the same fragile glue — a script here, a Zapier chain there — over and over, and break every time an app updated its API. Orbiq is the answer we wished existed: one visual flow builder, backed by real integrations we maintain for you.

Today we're a remote-first team of 34, headquartered in Chennai with people across four time zones, all still using the product daily to run our own company.
